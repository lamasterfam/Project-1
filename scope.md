# Project Scope Statement    
## Project Name: Balancing Family, Eduation, and Career
### Purpose 
* To ensure quality time is spent with family while also devoting quantifiable time toward education and career. 
### Description 
* The project would require creating routines for family
* Utilizing strong time managment for studies/education
* Discovering leaner ways to process workloads in career that encourage remote work
* Allocating Tue-Fri toward family sports, dinners, and chores
### Desired Results
* Ensure proper 7 hour sleep schedule to reduce the risk of burnout/exhaustion
* Allocate 20 hours per week of study time during breaks, lunches and children nap times or during their homework time
* Utilize work from home options to eliminate 20 hours of commute/work travel 
* Acknowledge childrens' milestones and attend one event per week per child
### Exclusions
* Full sleep cycle dependent on newborn sleep schedule
* Work from home options subjected to change to full in person
* Children events can sometimes overlap where more than one child has event at the same time as another child!
